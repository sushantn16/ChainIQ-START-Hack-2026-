Level 3 — Escalation & Outcomes
When Humans Step In
9 escalation rules are checked on every request. Only 2 are hard blocks. The pipeline always produces a usable result.
Escalation Rules
▼
RuleTriggerType
ER-002 Preferred supplier is policy-restricted Block
ER-003 Contract value in CPO-tier threshold Advisory
ER-004 No compliant supplier found — discovery triggered Advisory
ER-005 Data residency constraint cannot be satisfied Block
ER-006 Quantity exceeds supplier monthly capacity Advisory
ER-007 Influencer / Marketing — brand-safety review Advisory
ER-008 Delivery to regulated non-EU market Advisory
ER-LEAD All suppliers have infeasible lead times Advisory
ER-RISK Top-ranked supplier has elevated/high risk score Advisory
Only BLOCK escalations halt the decision. All advisory escalations are attached to the recommendation and routed to the right owner — the pipeline always produces a usable output.
Possible Outcomes
Recommend
No blocking escalations. Shortlist ranked, winner cited with price, quality score, risk tier, and historical track record. Full audit trail attached.
Recommend with Escalation
Advisory flags exist — high contract value, lead time pressure, capacity risk, or regulated market. Recommendation still issued. Human reviewer notified automatically.
Cannot Proceed
Compliance block triggered: restricted supplier selected, or data residency constraint unsatisfiable. Top-ranked alternative shown for when compliance is resolved.
Level 4 — Intelligence Layer
Smart Features
Beyond rule-matching — four capabilities that make the engine self-improving and auditable.
01
Autonomous Supplier Discovery
When fewer than 3 compliant suppliers are found, the best price exceeds budget by >20%, or a single-supplier dependency is detected — the engine automatically searches the web for new candidates, extracts them with LLM analysis, and surfaces unvetted options for onboarding review.
Live web search + LLM + market intelligence fallback
02
Iterative & Incomplete Input
Missing quantity, budget, or deadline never blocks the pipeline. The engine runs, produces a preview result, and returns one specific question per missing field — with a suggested value derived from the shortlist (e.g. "Budget suggestion: EUR 42,000 based on top-ranked supplier"). Free text in any language is translated automatically.
Self-healing · never dead-ends · multilingual
03
What-If Scenario Engine
After every decision the engine generates up to 6 concrete alternatives: raise budget to close the gap, reduce quantity to fit budget, extend the deadline to avoid expedited surcharges, split by country for more coverage, order more units to hit a volume tier, or show the exact cost of choosing the preferred supplier over the recommended one.
Up to 6 actionable scenarios per request · one-click apply
04
Audit-Ready Narrative
Every recommendation includes a plain-language explanation generated by Claude — why the winner ranked first, the cost of overriding it with a preferred supplier, lead time warnings, historical win rate and escalation context, and a full audit trail of every policy and supplier evaluated.
Claude-generated · explainable · procurement-ready
